Scoots is your go-to motor scooter rental company on the stunning island of Cozumel, nestled in the western Caribbean Sea. As a local, owner-operated business, we take pride in offering convenient and friendly services to everyone, from island residents to spontaneous walk-in tourists and avid motor scooter enthusiasts.

Choose from our diverse fleet, including motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs. We cater to your adventurous spirit, ensuring a memorable experience on the beautiful island.

Our Locations

El Centro de Cozumel: Near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock, our central location provides easy access for all visitors exploring the heart of Cozumel.

Terminal Puerta Maya: Located further south along the coast, near the cruise terminals, this location is perfect for cruise-based touring groups and partnerships.
